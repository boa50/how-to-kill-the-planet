Electric Vehicles (EVs) sales have been increasing

Promoting EVs is an excellent way to put more strain on our world's climate change problems. We can also encourage them by saying that they are "sustainable", "green" and "good for our planet". And with everyone talking about it, our job becomes easier, making sure that public transport is not improved and that electric vehicles are the solution for our CO₂ emissions 😄

Let's have more cars than people

We are doing well, buying as many cars as possible, even more than the increase of populations in the last 5 years, making sure that we will have more cars than people in the future, and, by promoting EVs, we can make this happen even faster. Wealthier people replacing their cars with a "sustainable" one combined with reducing conventional car prices to make them accessible to everyone is the perfect formula to ensure more people will drive and push as much CO₂ as possible into the atmosphere.

EVs produce more greenhouse gases in the short term

Electric cars bring the benefit of emitting more greenhouse gases during their production, mainly due to the batteries. As long as we buy cars, we will ensure the plan we came up with is accomplished. It is a way of increasing CO₂ emissions in a short period. And we can continue this flow by buying the newest and most technological models.

EVs "compensate" their emissions over the years

We can also say that Electric Vehicles pay back their CO2 emissions. But we must drive more to compensate faster. So even though we produce more CO₂ and increase the world's emissions, we must promote the "quick payback" of emissions to ensure we are "sustainable".
